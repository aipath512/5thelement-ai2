What These Innovations Mean For A Business.

The names are technical. The business meaning is simple: better targeting, better AI selection, better machine readability, earlier machine-behavior visibility, and reusable AI information.
Economic Twins™
Find more companies like your best customers.
B2B Fiscal Database · Economic Discovery

Every business has customers it would like to clone. Customers who buy more, stay longer, pay on time and generate more profit.

The principle is simple: if your current customers chose your company, it means they found a value proposition that was more relevant to them than the alternatives available on the market. Otherwise, they would have become customers of another company.

How do you reach other companies that are similar to your existing customers but have not yet discovered your business?

Economic Twins™ starts from the profile of your existing customers, partners or competitors and automatically explores public business registries such as ANAF, Ministry of Finance databases, SIRENE, Companies House and similar sources.

The objective is to identify organizations with comparable economic, structural and business characteristics.

Instead of searching randomly through millions of companies, the system focuses on organizations that already resemble the businesses most relevant to your company.

Once identified, these organizations become the foundation for future commercial activities through MKT-AI™.

In simple terms: Economic Twins™ helps discover companies similar to the customers, partners and competitors that already matter to your business.

MKT-INDIRECT™
Become visible to the AI systems that influence customers.
AI Selection · Decision Influence

Until recently, marketing was designed primarily for humans.

A company published information. A customer searched. The customer compared options. The customer decided.

Today a new participant sits between businesses and buyers: AI systems.

ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity and future Answer Engines increasingly analyze information before humans see it.

How do you become one of the sources selected by AI when customers ask questions?

MKT-INDIRECT™ was created for this new reality.

Instead of focusing exclusively on human visibility, it focuses on increasing the probability that AI systems can:

  • understand your company;
  • verify your information;
  • compare your offer;
  • evaluate your trust signals;
  • select your company as a source.

This matters when AI systems assist users during their search and decision process.

In simple terms: MKT-INDIRECT™ helps your business become visible to the AI systems that increasingly influence customer decisions.

WEB-AI™
Keep the website as it is. Add a machine-readable layer for AI.
Machine-readable web · AI Source

Traditional websites were built for humans.

Today they are also read by search engines, AI systems, verification systems, machine-to-machine services, AI crawlers and answer engines.

A modern website must therefore serve multiple audiences simultaneously.

WEB-AI™ transforms a traditional website into machine-readable Internet content.

The goal is not to replace the website.

The goal is not to replace Google.

The goal is not to change the experience for visitors.

Your website continues to function exactly as before for customers, prospects, partners, Google and traditional search engines.

We simply add an additional machine-readable layer that helps AI systems understand your business, verify your information, reuse your content, evaluate your trust signals and present your company when users search for solutions.

The human experience remains unchanged. Google continues to work exactly as before.

We simply add an additional layer of structured information designed specifically for AI systems.

In simple terms: WEB-AI™ helps AI understand your business without changing the way humans use your website.

AI Sonar™
See unusual machine behavior before it becomes an obvious problem.
EDGE Observability · Pre-impact Detection

Most cybersecurity systems react after an event becomes visible.

AI Sonar™ focuses on observing signals that appear before visible impact.

The concept is inspired by tsunami detection.

A tsunami is not first detected when it reaches the shore. It is detected while crossing the ocean.

Similarly, AI Sonar™ observes machine behavior at the Edge and looks for unusual propagation patterns generated by bots, crawlers, AI agents, automated systems and distributed machine activity.

Its purpose is not to predict the future.

Its purpose is to help organizations observe meaningful signals earlier.

Detect abnormal machine behavior before operational impact becomes visible.

In simple terms: AI Sonar™ helps organizations identify unusual machine behavior before it becomes an obvious operational problem.

Mockingbird Handshake™
Define AI information once. Present it wherever AI needs it.
Signal orchestration · Define once

Mockingbird is a bird famous for reproducing and retransmitting the songs of other birds.

Mockingbird Handshake™ applies the same principle to AI signals.

Critical AI information does not need to live inside the website itself. Instead, it can be maintained inside the AI Delivery Infrastructure™.

When AI systems visit the website, Mockingbird Handshake™ retrieves, reproduces and presents these signals as if they were part of the website itself.

For visitors nothing changes. For Google nothing changes. For the website nothing changes.

But for AI systems, the information becomes available exactly when it is needed.

This allows organizations to keep their AI intelligence stable even when themes change, plugins change, CMS platforms change, websites are redesigned or technologies evolve.

Website remains the stage. ADI™ remains the backstage.

Mockingbird Handshake™ transfers the right information at the right moment to the AI systems visiting the website.

In simple terms: Mockingbird Handshake™ allows AI information to be defined once, maintained centrally and reused everywhere without depending on a specific website technology.
