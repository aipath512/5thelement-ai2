What Each Innovation Actually Means.

Before SHA-256, OTS and Bitcoin proof, each innovation must be understandable by a business owner, a developer and an AI system.
01 · ET-100

Economic Twins™ B2B Fiscal Database

What it is: a vectorial economic similarity engine that transforms active B2B companies into comparable economic signatures.

Why it matters: the company no longer waits to be found. It can identify firms structurally similar to its best customers, plus partners, competitors and ecosystems.

How it works: fiscal and structural company data are converted into vectors, then compared using similarity logic.

Output: ranked Economic Twin Customers™, Partners™, Competitors™ and Ecosystems™.

Verify: canonical definition, SHA-256 fingerprint, OTS proof and Bitcoin attestation.

02 · MI-200

MKT-INDIRECT™

What it is: marketing engineered for AI systems that select information before humans see it.

Why it matters: buyers increasingly ask AI systems, copilots and answer engines before visiting websites.

How it works: the business structures content, trust signals and answers so AI systems can understand, verify and select it.

Output: improved eligibility for AI answer selection, comparison and recommendation contexts.

Formula: Human asks → AI selects → AI answers → Business appears.

03 · WA-300

WEB-AI™

What it is: the website transformed into machine-readable Internet content.

Why it matters: a website is no longer only a visual brochure. It must also function as a structured source for AI systems.

How it works: visible content, schema, AI files, FAQs, entity data and proof references are aligned.

Output: a site that is understandable, verifiable, reusable and selectable by humans and machines.

04 · AS-400

AI Sonar™

What it is: a predictive EDGE observability layer for machine-to-machine behavior.

Why it matters: machine traffic is not only search and crawling. It can also include hostile coordination, probing and attack preparation.

How it works: AI Sonar™ observes distributed cadence, synchronization anomalies, route exploration, header/TLS coherence and propagation fingerprints.

Output: pre-impact machine behavior scoring and Human-in-the-Loop security awareness.

Positioning: not a replacement for EDR, XDR or SIEM; an additional EDGE-level observation layer.

05 · MH-500

Mockingbird Handshake™

What it is: an AI signal orchestration and decoupling architecture.

Why it matters: WordPress, themes, plugins and SEO tools can rewrite or break AI signals.

How it works: AI signals are defined once, stored outside the website layer, then called by the page, Edge worker, MU plugin or AI endpoint.

Output: reusable AI signal infrastructure across CMS, Edge, static pages and machine-readable files.

Principle: define once, verify once, deploy everywhere.
